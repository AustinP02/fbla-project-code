Welcome, Employers! This is your hub for posting job opportunities and discovering motivated students from Passaic County Technical-Vocational Schools (PCTVS). Let us help you find the best talent for your business

How It Works

A simple breakdown of how employers can use the platform:

  • Post a Job: Submit job opportunities by filling out a form with the position's details.
  • Job Approval: Our team will review and approve your job posting, ensuring it meets our guidelines.
  • Connect with Students: Once approved, your job will be displayed on the student-facing job board, and you'll begin receiving applications from interested students.
Benefits of working with PCTVS students!
  • Access to motivated and skilled students from a variety of vocational programs.
  • Ability to provide real-world experience to students while benefiting from fresh perspectives and energy.
  • Direct engagement with the next generation of professionals in tech, engineering, business, and more.
