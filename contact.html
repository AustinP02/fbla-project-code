When Should You Contact Us?

You might want to contact us if:

Students:

  • If you need help creating or improving your resume.
  • If you are unsure that you meet all the requirements to have a job.
  • If you have any general inquiries.
  • If you're unsure how to apply for a job listed on our site.
  • If you suspect a job listing is fraudulent or misleading.
  • If you want to report inappropriate content or behavior.
  • If you find any issues with this website.
  • If you would like to no longer recieve our newsletter.

Employers:

  • If you want to post a job opportunity for students.
  • If you need help managing or editing your job listings.
  • If you're unsure whether your job listing meets our guidelines.
  • If you want to promote or feature your job posting.
  • If you suspect fraudulent applications or misuse of your listing.
  • If you want to report inappropriate content or behavior.
  • If you find any issues with this website.
  • If you need assistance with hiring best practices for student employees.
